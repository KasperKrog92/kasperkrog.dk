Kartoteket — the archive

Five rooms, doors open.

Projects and worlds, in no particular order of importance. Each one is alive in its own way.

N°01

Aarhus Gamestormers gamestormers.dk

A community-driven video game club in Aarhus that treats games as cultural experiences worth talking about — closer to a film club than a tournament. Stories, shared evenings, and room for curious people.

community · games as culture · storytelling · belonging

↗ N°02

Turkis Crew turkis.gamestormers.dk

A volunteer platform for cultural spaces — software shaped around people instead of metrics. Built for music venues and the humans who keep them running, on trust and accessibility rather than dashboards.

humane technology · volunteers · cultural infrastructure

↗ N°03

Matchabladet matchabladet.dk

A Danish matcha journal about ritual, slowness and attention. Part café guide, part reflective blog, part aesthetic archive — tea as a calm anchor in a loud world.

ritual · slowness · cafés · attention

↗ N°04

Solis Lantern Chronicles solis.gamestormers.dk

An evolving, slightly mythological creative space — mood, identity, systems and worldbuilding. Less a product than a lantern carried through liminal rooms.

worldbuilding · liminal spaces · experiment · introspection

↗ N°05

Aarhus Folk Festival folk.gamestormers.dk

A redesign concept for a community-driven folk music festival in Aarhus: warm, alive and intergenerational. Music as shared human experience, not commercial spectacle.

folk culture · gathering · tradition · scandinavian warmth
