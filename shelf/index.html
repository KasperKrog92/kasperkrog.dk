on the shelf.

What's feeding the atmosphere lately: books, sounds, ideas, leaves. The shelf changes with the weather. That's the point of it.

Things on the shelf

game

Disco Elysium

A detective story where the hardest case is being a person. Still unmatched, word for word.

books

Malazan Book of the Fallen

Ten volumes of grief and compassion at geological scale. I'm on the seventh now, Reaper's Gale, still deep in the middle of it.

sound

Trip hop

A slow trance to focus inside. Beautiful melancholy, with determination running under it.

dance

Folk dancing

Going to balls, turning with strangers until the room moves as one body. I come for the laughter and the brief feeling of a crowd thinking together.

film

Slow cinema

Films that trust you to wait. The long take as an act of respect.

image

Rainy city photography

Neon on wet asphalt. The city developing itself like film.

leaf

Matcha

Good powder does more than hold off tiredness. It sharpens focus and opens up how much of the world I can feel.

idea

Process philosophy & Daoism

Change is the only constant; everything is mid-transition. Meaning is a process, with no clean start and no end.

sound

On repeat lately

Otha, Jungle, Low Roar, Daphni: songs built to move to, with a thread of melancholy through the best of them.

the shelf rotates, and sometimes spirals back.
